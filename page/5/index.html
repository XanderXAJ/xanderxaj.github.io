Torchure for Android

  Posted on July 14, 2010
Ever found yourself in need of a torch, but only had your phone handy? Look no further! Torchure (for Android) is a pretty simple application. It turns the screen white and, as default, whacks up the backlight to maximum. I did say simple, didn’t I? However, it also lets you change that backlight brightness (useful if, say, you’ve been to a particularly wild party and you need to step over some people without waking/blinding them) and lock it so you don’t go accidentally changing it.
